Nandos


Store info:Nando’s is a South African multinational chain that specializes in Portuguese flame-grilled peri-peri style chicken. Founded in Johannesburg in 1987, it has grown to operate over 1,200 outlets in 30 countries. The brand’s logo features the Rooster of Barcelos, a common symbol of Portugal. The restaurant was initially named Chickenland but was renamed Nando’s after one of the founder’s sons.
