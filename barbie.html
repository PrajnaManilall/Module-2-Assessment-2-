Barbie

Actors:Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling, Issa Rae

Age Restrictions:PG-13

Showing times:8:00- 10:00, 12:00-14:00, 21:00-23:00

Synopsis:The Barbie movie is about Barbie and Ken travelling to the real world in Los Angeles. Barbie, voiced by Margot Robbie, is the most popular of all the Barbies in Barbieland and begins experiencing an existential crisis. She must travel to the human world in order to understand herself and discover her true purpose. Throughout the film, Greta Gerwig artfully blends comedy, emotional depth, and tongue-in-cheek dialogue to explore the complexities of being a teenage girl and the various challenges and aspirations that come with it
